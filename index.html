Based in the underground of the city, Nightline Tattooer is a one-chair private studio dedicated to precise, intentional work. Every session is a collaboration — your vision, refined through years of craft.

Specialising in fine-line, blackwork, and dark realism, the studio accepts a limited number of clients per month to ensure every piece receives undivided attention.
